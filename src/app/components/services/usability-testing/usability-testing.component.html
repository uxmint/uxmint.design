Why Usability Testing?

It helps the business to evaluates the product or website before it is launched in the market by testing it with a sample population [small group of actual users].

How Usability Testing is performed?

Task Planning

The scenarios for testing are planned along with the environment and the resultant parameters

Recuit Users

Ideal users of the product or website are defined and recruited to suite the different personas

Test Execution

Users are briefed on the tasks and are recorded

Analysis

The task outcome, observations, physical gestures recorded are analysed

Test Report

Summary of outcome are comparitively reported

Recommendations

Recommendations and feature enhancements based on the user expectations are reported to the business team
