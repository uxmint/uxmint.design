We created Brand Identity for UX Ahead and responsive website that addresses its business to business capabilities.

About Project

Ux ahead is a design studio based out of London, They expertise in creating seamless customer experience for online business and also for businesses with physical presence. Services they offer are Branding, Digital marketing strategy, Development and online reputation management.

Our goal was to communicate all of the mentioned things to their clients who are businesses that deal with the end customers of the service or the product. And also create a branding strategy and a visual language for will make UX Ahead that would make it recognisable in the industry

Branding

The primary goal of the branding exercise was to create an identity that shows focus and progress. For which we started with the kickoff discussion about the vision of the brand. There after going about creating the Moodboard for inspiration, brainstorming and design direction. Our process helped us to come up with multiple variations for the 'Jewel' in the combination mark and to pick the right typeface and colour family for the brand
