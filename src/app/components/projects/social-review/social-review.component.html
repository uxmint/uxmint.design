Discovery and Analysis

We started noticing a pattern among the users interviews we conducted. Every user persona had its own Frustrations, Challenges, Breaking points, etc,. All of which had to be addressed in the format that will be acceptable by all the user segments.

We started from "Zero". Knowing how word of mouth spreads was worth probing the chapters of history. Discoveries were quite interesting based on why people follow certain behaviour of promoting Product/services organically instead of doing it online.
