Health Buy

A startup focused on SaaS solutions that empower local communities, globally for healthy food produce

About Project

A platform to connect local farming communities to needy urban dwellers to bring a impact in Health Sector.

The application connect various segments of users ranging from home-makers, organic produce users, concerned parents to the farmers who are suppressed by the middle-men.
