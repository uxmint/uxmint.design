EVENT

MANAGEMENT

Platform for Venues Owners & Professionals to showcase their Venues and Services and find their customers online.

ABOUT PROJECT

Business segments like Hotel Management, Wedding Halls, Decorators, Photographers, Make-up Artists, Fashion Designers lean more and more into social media (Instagram & Facebook) to showcase their professional services.
But they lack the features to get requirements, coordinate with customers, provide quote, sign SLA and deliver seamless service.

Marriages and family events are more emotionally connected to people's lives in India; and the current generation is ready to outsource the events and sit back to enjoy a peaceful event without the hassles of organising themselves. Connecting the dots between these two segments is the role of the platform.
