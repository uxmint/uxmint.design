AUTOMOBILE USABILITY TESTING

A multi-national automobile manufacturing company approached us for Usability Testing of alert system for their luxury segment vehicles to be launched Asian Market.

ABOUT PROJECT

An initial Pilot Testing was conducted with fewer users to understand the scenarios in which the prototype was put to use and the results were used to create detailed scenarios for different user segment in real-time scenario.

On the iterated version several participants were asked to test the prototype; the analysis and the results varied for different user segments.

Goal

Primary objective of this usability testing of prototype system was to observe the Behaviour under different conditions in target user segments; with the parameters of Cognition, Acceptance and Reach. How Culture & Environment impacts the understanding of the functionalities?

Understanding the preference of end-users for "Alert System" in terms of "Visual", "Audio" and "Haptics".

TESTING PROCESS

Testing Environment

Vehicle was tested in Natural Environment (to the extent possible) in calm road and broad daylight condition; while other pedestrians/vehicles were passing by the testing vehicle.

Equipment

Vehicle was provided with the prototype installed. Prototype was simulated for different scenarios with various alerts "Visual", "Audio" and "Haptics".

Testing Summary

The various touch points on user preferences, cultural impact, segment variance and the enhancements required were provided based on the exhaustive usability testing done on different user segments ranging from children, teenagers, adults, parents and elderly.
