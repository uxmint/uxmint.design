Digital Transformation for India's largest telecom network service provider involved redesigning the experience for customers, SMEs and business users. It involved breaking down complex manual process of quote, billing & invoicing that spanned across multiple platforms and CRM systems with high level of scalability & seamless customer experience.

About Project

We were approached with redesign of their legacy system that required change in process from the ground-up. The portal was intertwined with multiple other platforms that was feeding inputs to the UI. The Dashboards were a combination of financial and techincal data involving high cognition and technical knowledge for the end-users. It was a challenge to break down the functionalities into atomic structures to ensure ease of use and reusability for the UI Development.
