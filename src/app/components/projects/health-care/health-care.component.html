HEALTH CARE SYSTEM

Holistic healthcares system for the healthcare centers in remote hamlets to enable doctors, healthcare workers. Adopted by Government of Tamil Nadu for implementation across all the primay healthcare centers.

ABOUT PROJECT

An NGO approached us for Usability Testing of existing platform designed for Remote Healthcare Centers.

But as it spanned out the Usability Testing revealed deeper changes that were required and it turned out to be a full fledged User Experience Design revamp of the project.

RESEARCH

Understanding the existing

We landed ourselves in the clinics of the Andipatti, Thanjavur to get to know the day-to-day activities of the healthcare workers and the current system.

User interviews and Discoveries

The desktop system was consuming most of the time of the doctors in locating the patient record (finding the previous visit history, ailments and their medication). Also rather than allowing the doctors to diagonse; the system was suggesting with possible reasons for the patient's visit. There by reducing the interaction with the patient that eventually reduced the trust factor on doctors.

This was the driving factor for us, we had to change the process and reduce the overload for the doctors enabling better patient and doctor rapport at the same time collecting the data crucial for the management team.

SOLUTION

Our solutions enabled the doctors to interact with the patients reducing the effort spent in data retrieval and data entry for doctors. The field agents had to re-enter twice since they were in remote area that had very least connectivity. As part of the solution the devices “Tablets” that they carried were loaded with updates in Wi-Fi enabled zones of the clinic everyday before and after their field visit reducing effort data entry and paper work.

We brought in changes in process that allowed data entry to be taken up by other staff members who were part of the primary healthcare and were localites who knew the patients history and their frequency of visits.

When we were requested to be part of the launch event it was indeed a suprise that it was taken over by Government of Tamil Nadu. An honour that we need expected when we started it a Usability Testing activity.
