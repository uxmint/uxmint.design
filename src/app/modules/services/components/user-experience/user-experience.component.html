Our Process

Stake Holder Discussion

We start our requirements gathering from the pillars of the business; the Product Owners, Business SME, CEO/CTO to get their perspectives, needs & current process issues that they encounter and the potential areas of improvement. Based on the inputs we define the features and the scope.
On a broader scope the vision for the product and the market context gets defined. Next step would be SWAT Analysis on the competitors in the market.

Discovering User Needs

We funnel the requirements from the Stakeholders, Business Team and the End-Users - align according to the Vision of the Product. It takes a series of process to get the inference from the Research Data. Based on the inputs gleaned from the extensive research and analysis we plot the Journey Maps & Persona. This allowing us to Empathise with the end-users bringing value to the Design Strategy.

The Divergent & Convergent Thinking Process aims to unearth the innovative solutions and to nail the needs & challenges of the end-users & the business.

1. User Research
2. User Persona
3. Users Journey Map
4. Root Cause Analysis

Supporting Solutions

We combine the research and the insights to conceptualize to build the wireframes and iterate the prototypes with the business, technical team and the consumers.

1. Conceptual Model
2. Task Flow
3. Card Sorting
4. Information Architecture

Test Feature Viability

We value your time & effort, we employ Early Testing to avoid rework due to mismatch between the users/market needs. We design Visual Prototype and test in multiple stages. Iterate design based on the feedback avoiding changes during the product development or even worse after market launch which could be very expensive for the business.

1. Low Fidelity Wireframe
2. High Fidelity Wireframe
3. Clickable Prototyping
4. Iteration
