Job Description

The candidate would be responsible for creating posters for e-commerce brands for Web & Responsive platform. Need to work cross-functionally, to understand the needs from the brand managers, translate ideas to visuals and also coax with development team.

Responsibilities

- Creating banner images, marketing material for Retail Brands by following brand guidelines
- Perform retouching and manipulation of images
- Collaborate with the team to ensure consistency of designs across various media outlets
- Create compelling and effective logos, designs in digital media
- Maintain awareness of current industry and technology standards, social media, competitive landscape and market trends

Skills

- Proven graphic designing experience [3-6 years]
- A strong portfolio of banner design, typography and illustrations
- Familiarity with design software and technologies (such as Photoshop, Sketch, InDesign, Illustrator)
- A keen eye for aesthetics and details
- Strong communication skills
- Ability to work methodically and meet deadlines

Optional Skills

- Degree in Design, Fine Arts or related field is a plus - Exposure in Retail domain added advantage - Understanding of HTML/CSS is a plus
