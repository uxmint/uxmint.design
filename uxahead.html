About Project
UXAHEAD is a design studio based out of London, they expertise in creating seamless customer experience for online business and also for businesses with physical presence. Services they offer are Branding, Digital Marketing Strategy, Development and Online Reputation Management.
Approach
Our goal was to communicate all of the mentioned things to their clients who are businesses that deal with the end customers of the service or the product
Target Audience
Local businesses, Professional Consultants, Online business, Design Team, Store Shops.
Strategy
Helping Local and International business to get better user and customer experience providing tailored UX Consulting and Digital media marketing to get most out of their online channel.
Converting qualitative data collected Stake holder Interview into design solutions
With the gathered data, We go ahead with the process of Information architecture, Task-flows, Wire frames, prototyping, Brand Identity to match our conceptual model to user’s mental models.
